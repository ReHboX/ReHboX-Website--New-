About us

ReHboX your life with AI.

ReHboX is an AI-powered digital therapeutics company that offers personalized & fun-filled exercise based therapies to people with acute & chronic Musculoskeletal conditions such as (neck, shoulder, chronic back, hip & knee pain).

ReHboX is revolutionizing the process of home exercise therapy; Providing Physiotherapists with the tool needed to recommend home exercise programs; track patients/ clients compliance and educating patients on their various health conditions.
